## 3. Introduction of the EU SME Centre（固定话术，按时长选版本）

**完整版**（hybrid/线下或时间充裕时）：

> Before we get started, let me take just a moment to briefly introduce the EU SME Centre.
>
> We're an EU-funded initiative, dedicated to supporting European small and medium-sized enterprises as you navigate the Chinese market. The EU SME Centre was set up back in 2010, and we've successfully completed four project phases—now we're thrilled to be in our fifth phase. Our core goal is to help SMEs from EU Member States and countries participating in the Single Market Programme explore and succeed in the Chinese market.
>
> We have four consortium partners: the European Union Chamber of Commerce in China, the China-Italian Chamber of Commerce, the Italy China Council Foundation, and SPI. We also have two associated partners: Trade Promotion Europe and the EU-China Business Association.
>
> And we provide five free frontline services for all of you:
>
> - **Our self-diagnosis tool**: You can fill out a short survey on our website to test your business's readiness for the Chinese market, and the tool will connect you with all the key knowledge and reports you need.
> - **Knowledge resources**: comprehensive industry reports, in-depth market guidelines, and real-life case studies to help you truly understand the Chinese market.
> - **Free expert advice**: You can reach out to us with any questions about doing business in China, and our team of experts and business advisors will give you professional, tailored suggestions.
> - **Training programmes**: This event is a perfect example of that! We host online, offline, and hybrid workshops, bringing together industry experts and practitioners to share in-depth, practical insights about the Chinese market.
> - **Advocacy work**: We gather all the challenges and concerns you may face when operating in China, and we make sure your voices are heard by the relevant authorities, to help improve the overall business environment here.
>
> That's a quick overview of the EU SME Centre. We welcome you to reach out to us and make the most of these five free services we offer.

**精简版**（单讲者 webinar）：五项服务压缩成一段（见 Gen Y 范例第 8–11 段），并提及本场 cohost。

## 8. Q&A session

**开场两个变体：**

> *（已有观众提问）* We have already received some questions from the audience, so let me start with the first one.
>
> *（暂无提问）* While we are waiting for questions from the audience, I would like to start with one question from our side.

**Prepared questions**：每位讲者 4–6 个（站在零经验 SME 视角：最优先行动 / 常见错误 / 有限资源怎么办 / 如何区分 X 和 Y / 从哪个平台或步骤开始），外加收尾问题：

> If you had to summarise today's discussion into one key message for European SMEs, what would that be?

**Q&A 管理句式（实战验证过的三件套）：**

- *问题太宽泛：* "That's such an important question! To make the answer as practical as possible for everyone here, could you please specify a little? Are you asking about {{X}}, or {{Y}}?"
- *回答过细超时：* "Thank you for that deep dive—this is really valuable! Let's wrap this point up with one key takeaway for EU SMEs, though, as we have lots more great questions from the chat to get through."
- *跑题：* "That's an interesting point to explore! Let's circle back to our core focus for today, though—how does this apply to EU SMEs doing {{trade/investment}} in China? Let's keep it targeted for our audience."
- *最后几分钟：* "We have just 5 minutes left for our Q&A, so we'll take two more questions—{{one focused on X, and one on Y}}—to cover as many of our audience's needs as possible."
