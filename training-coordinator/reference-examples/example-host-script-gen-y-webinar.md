Today we are going to discuss this generation in China
China’s Gen Y is not the one-size-fits-all generation. Many earn more money but face plenty of life pressures. They love spending, yet shop selectively: some stick to budget and trusted brands, while others may spend on experiences they value.
For European SMEs, this is a very important point. The opportunity in China is still there, but the old assumptions may no longer work.
Today’s session will help us understand the psychology and behaviour of China’s Gen Y consumers, and more importantly, what these insights mean for European SMEs in practical market decisions.
